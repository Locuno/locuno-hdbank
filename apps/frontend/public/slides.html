Locuno: Engineering Trust

Community Wallet + Social Credit Score + ESG Transparency

The Problem

  • Families: Lack of a private, consent-based safety net.
  • Communities: Opaque fund management via Zalo & Excel, leading to mistrust.

The Solution: A Unified Trust Platform

  • Community Wallet: 2/3 Approval voting, real-time ledger, VietQR deposits.
  • Social Credit Score: Explainable 0-100 score unlocking bank-backed community credit.
  • ESG Transparency: Measurable Social + Governance impact with an auditable history.

Live Demo Flow

  1. Deposit via VietQR → Balance & Ledger Update.
  2. Score Recomputes → Shows Reasons & Improvement Tips.
  3. Apply for Loan → Auto-approved based on score.
  4. Disburse Funds → Repayments via "REPAY <id> <amount>".
  5. Create Spend Request → Executed after 2/3 Vote.
  6. ESG Card Updates → Participation & Governance metrics.

Technology & APIs

  • Architecture: Cloudflare Workers, Durable Objects, "Business Logic Blockchain".
  • Key APIs:
  • POST /communities/:id/credit/score
  • GET /communities/:id/credit/score
  • POST /communities/:id/loan/(apply | disburse | repay)
  • GET /communities/:id/loan/status

Value to Bank & Ecosystem

  • Drives new deposits and enables responsible credit expansion.
  • Provides transparent governance with measurable ESG impact.
  • Acts as a Loyalty OS for the entire Sovico ecosystem (HDBank, Vietjet, etc.).

Roadmap & Risk Mitigation

  • Day 1: End-to-end live demo.
  • Month 1: Pilots with residential communities and schools.
  • Quarter 2: Family SOS feature, richer social signals for scoring.
  • Risks: Privacy/bias, fraud, and governance risks mitigated via consent, eKYC, and transparent audit trails.

The Ask: Call to Action

Approve a pilot program to validate that trust is the most powerful driver of growth, loyalty, and financial inclusion.
